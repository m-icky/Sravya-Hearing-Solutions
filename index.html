About Sravya
Hearing Solutions

At Sravya Hearing Solutions, we believe better hearing transforms lives. Our mission is to provide accurate hearing assessments, personalized recommendations, advanced hearing aid technology, and lifelong support in a comfortable, patient-focused environment.

Located in Thrissur, Kerala, we serve patients across the region with compassion, expertise, and the very best in modern audiological care. Every patient who walks through our doors receives our complete attention and a solution built entirely around their unique needs.

How It Works

Our Simple
4-Step Process

From your first call to lifelong hearing wellness — we make it simple, comfortable, and completely guided.

1

Book Your Free Consultation

Call, WhatsApp, or walk in to schedule your first appointment. No referral needed. No fees. We'll confirm a time that suits you and let you know what to expect — so you arrive feeling at ease.

Book via WhatsApp
2

Comprehensive Hearing Evaluation

Our specialist conducts a full audiological assessment using calibrated equipment. We measure your hearing across all frequencies, identify the type and degree of any loss, and explain the results in plain language.

3

Personalised Device Trial

Based on your assessment, we recommend two or three hearing aids from different brands and price points. You trial each one — in the clinic and in your real daily life — with no obligation to purchase.

4

Fitting, Fine-Tuning & Ongoing Care

Once you choose your device, we fit and digitally program it to your precise hearing profile. Follow-up appointments ensure the sound is exactly right as you adjust — and we remain available for support, servicing, and check-ins long after.
